About the Hackathon

Innovating for safer roads through advanced driver monitoring systems

Objective

SafeDrive Hack 2025 challenges participants to develop innovative solutions for driver monitoring systems that enhance road safety. Using AI, computer vision, and sensor technologies, teams will create systems to detect and alert for various driver behaviors and road conditions.

Our mission is to reduce road accidents by 30% through technological innovation and collaboration between students, industry experts, and transportation authorities.

Prizes & Sponsorship

Sponsored by DIET-Technology Business Incubator, the hackathon offers exciting prizes for the most innovative and effective solutions. Winners will receive cash prizes, incubation opportunities, and industry recognition.

1st Prize

₹50,000 + Incubation

2nd Prize

₹30,000 + Mentorship

3rd Prize

₹20,000 + Resources

Scope & Impact

Solutions developed during the hackathon will be evaluated for real-world implementation in public transportation systems. The APSRTC has expressed interest in adopting the most promising technologies to enhance driver safety across their fleet.

Winning teams will have the opportunity to work with transportation authorities to refine and deploy their solutions, making a tangible impact on road safety across the region.

Design Thinking Process

Our structured approach to innovation and problem-solving

1

EMPATHIZE

Understand users' needs and challenges through observation and engagement

2

DEFINE

Clearly articulate the problem based on insights gathered during empathy phase

3

IDEATE

Generate a wide range of creative solutions through brainstorming and collaboration

4

PROTOTYPE

Build tangible representations of ideas to explore potential solutions

5

TEST

Evaluate prototypes with users, gather feedback, and refine solutions

6

IMPLEMENT

Develop final solution and prepare for real-world deployment
